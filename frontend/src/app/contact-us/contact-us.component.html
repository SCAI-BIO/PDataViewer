Prof. Dr. Martin Hofmann Apitius

Head of the Bioinformatics Department

Martin is leading the Department of Bioinformatics at the Fraunhofer Institute for Algorithms and Scientific Computing (SCAI) in Sankt Augustin (Germany), a governmental not-for-profit, applied research institute. He is also Professor for Applied Life Science Informatics at Bonn-Aachen International Center for Information Technology (B-IT). He is (co-) author of more than 250 scientific publications.

Marc is leading the Software and Scientific Computing Team and also the Deputy Head of Business Area Bioinformatics at the Fraunhofer Institute for Algorithms and Scientific Computing (SCAI) in Sankt Augustin (Germany), a governmental not-for-profit, applied research institute.

Dr. Yasamin Salimi

Postdoctoral Researcher

Yasamin joined Fraunhofer SCAI in 2019, where she completed her master's thesis and PhD studies in biomedical data science. As a postdoctoral researcher, she focuses on data integration, patient stratification, predictive modeling, and federated learning in healthcare. In the EU project CERTAINTY, she develops AI models to support individualized treatment decisions and advance precision medicine.
