PDataViewer
Exploring the Parkinson's Disease Data Landscape

ABOUT

Data collected in cohort studies lay the groundwork for a plethora of Parkinson's disease (PD) research endeavors. PDataViewer lets you explore this PD data landscape and identify cohort datasets that suit your research needs.

We accessed and curated major PD cohort datasets in a purely data-driven manner with the aim of 1) characterizing their underlying data, 2) assessing the quantity and availability of data, and 3) evaluating the interoperability across these distinct cohort datasets.
